IMDb Rating vs Rotten Tomatoes Rating

The highest rated movie on IMDb movie with a high corresponding Rotten Tomatoes score is "The Dark Knight," an Action/Adventure movie currently only being streamed on Hulu.

Rotten Tomatoes has a number of movies rated 99%. This includes movies such as "Parasite," "Shoplifters," "Gloria," "McQueen" and "Truman."

The visualisation shows that the movies with a shorter runtime tend to score a lower IMDb rating. Additionally, it is shown that from 1999, the number of movies with a large runtime of 200+ has decreased significantly.
